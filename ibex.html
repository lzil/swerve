Team: Kevin Ng (PM), Liang Zhou (Architect), Manting Lao, Hassan Kane, Balint Gyimesi (Google Advisor)

Swerve is an Android application promoting live social networking and activity sharing. With Swerve, we are making it easier for users to share their location and activities with their friends and the people surrounding them. Swerve serves as a platform where people can share their activities and broadcasting the information to their friends easily. With this in mind, we designed Swerve to allow users to be able to create activity updates and seamlessly see what their friends are up to.
